# Past Tenses

## Overview

Past tenses dalam bahasa Inggris digunakan untuk membicarakan kejadian, keadaan, atau proses yang terjadi di masa lalu. Ada **4 bentuk past tense** yang masing-masing memiliki fungsi berbeda — bukan sekadar "sudah terjadi", tapi **bagaimana** dan **dalam konteks apa** kejadian itu berlangsung.

---

## 1. Simple Past

### Definisi
Menyatakan **aksi atau keadaan yang terjadi dan selesai di waktu yang spesifik di masa lalu**. Waktu kejadian biasanya disebutkan secara eksplisit atau jelas dari konteks.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + V2 (past form)
```
> *She **worked** late yesterday. / They **went** to Bali last week.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + did not (didn't) + base verb
```
> *She **didn't work** yesterday. / They **didn't go** to Bali.*

**Interrogative**
```
Did + subject + base verb?
```
> ***Did** she **work** yesterday? / **Did** they **go** to Bali?*

**Verb To Be**
```
Positive   : Subject + was / were
Negative   : Subject + was/were + not
Interrogative : Was/Were + subject?
```
> *He **was** tired. / They **were** late. / **Was** he tired?*

**Regular vs Irregular Verbs**

| Jenis | Aturan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| Regular | Tambah **-ed** | work → worked, play → played |
| Berakhir -e | Tambah **-d** | live → lived, love → loved |
| Konsonan + y | Ganti **y → ied** | study → studied, try → tried |
| CVC + satu suku kata | Gandakan konsonan + **-ed** | stop → stopped, plan → planned |
| Irregular | Hafal V2-nya | go → went, buy → bought, write → wrote |

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aksi yang Selesai di Waktu Spesifik di Masa Lalu**
> *I **visited** Paris in 2019.*
> *She **called** me at 8 PM last night.*

**b. Serangkaian Aksi Berurutan di Masa Lalu**
> *He **woke up**, **took** a shower, and **left** the house.*

**c. Kebiasaan atau Keadaan di Masa Lalu (yang sudah tidak berlaku)**
> *We **lived** in Bandung when I was a child.*
> *She **walked** to school every day when she was young.*

**d. Durasi Tertentu di Masa Lalu (yang sudah selesai)**
> *I **worked** there for 5 years.* *(dan sekarang sudah tidak bekerja di sana)*

---

### Signal Words
`yesterday` · `last night/week/month/year` · `in 2010` · `ago` · `in the past` · `when I was young` · `once` · `at that time` · `then` · `the other day`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Memakai base verb (V1) di kalimat positif**
- ❌ *She go to school yesterday.*
- ✅ *She **went** to school yesterday.*

**2. Memakai V2 setelah did**
- ❌ *Did she **went** to school?*
- ✅ *Did she **go** to school?*

**3. Memakai didn't + V2**
- ❌ *She didn't **worked** yesterday.*
- ✅ *She didn't **work** yesterday.*

**4. Bingung was vs were**
- ❌ *They was late.*
- ✅ *They **were** late.*

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Jika ada **waktu spesifik** di kalimat (*yesterday, in 1990, last year, ago*), jawaban hampir pasti **Simple Past** — bukan Present Perfect.
- TOEFL sering menguji **irregular verb forms**. Pastikan hafal V2 umum: *go → went, write → wrote, take → took, break → broke, know → knew*.
- Dalam **error identification**, kesalahan paling umum adalah *did + V2* atau *didn't + V2* — selalu cek bentuk verb setelah did/didn't.

## 2. Past Continuous (Past Progressive)

### Definisi
Menyatakan **aksi yang sedang berlangsung pada suatu momen tertentu di masa lalu**, atau **aksi yang sedang berjalan ketika aksi lain terjadi**.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + was / were + verb-ing
```
> *I **was working** at 9 PM last night.*
> *They **were playing** football when it rained.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + was / were + not + verb-ing
```
> *She **wasn't sleeping** when I called.*

**Interrogative**
```
Was / Were + subject + verb-ing?
```
> ***Was** she **sleeping** when you arrived?*

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aksi yang Sedang Berlangsung pada Waktu Tertentu di Masa Lalu**
> *At 10 PM last night, I **was studying**.*
> *What **were** you **doing** at noon yesterday?*

**b. Aksi yang Sedang Berjalan Ketika Aksi Lain Terjadi (+ Simple Past)**
> *I **was cooking** when the phone **rang**.*
> *She **was walking** home when it **started** to rain.*

> Pola klasik: **Past Continuous** (aksi berlangsung) + **when** + **Simple Past** (aksi yang menyela)

**c. Dua Aksi yang Berlangsung Bersamaan di Masa Lalu**
> *While she **was reading**, he **was watching** TV.*

**d. Latar Belakang Suasana dalam Cerita (Background Setting)**
> *The sun **was setting**, birds **were singing**, and the village **was slowly waking** up.*

---

### Signal Words
`when` · `while` · `at that moment` · `at 9 PM last night` · `at that time` · `as` · `just as`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Bingung kapan pakai Past Continuous vs Simple Past**

> Gunakan **Past Continuous** untuk aksi yang sedang berjalan (background).
> Gunakan **Simple Past** untuk aksi yang menyela atau terjadi secara tiba-tiba.

- ❌ *I cooked when the phone rang.* *(ambigu — terkesan dua aksi berurutan)*
- ✅ *I **was cooking** when the phone **rang**.* *(memasak sedang berlangsung, lalu telepon berdering)*

**2. Memakai stative verbs dalam Past Continuous**
- ❌ *She was knowing the answer.*
- ✅ *She **knew** the answer.*

**3. Salah menentukan which action uses which tense**
- Aksi lebih panjang → Past Continuous
- Aksi lebih pendek / menyela → Simple Past

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Pola **"was/were + V-ing ... when ... V2"** sangat sering muncul di TOEFL. Pahami baik-baik mana aksi latar dan mana aksi penyela.
- Kata **`while`** hampir selalu berpasangan dengan Past Continuous; kata **`when`** bisa berpasangan dengan keduanya — perhatikan konteksnya.
- **Stative verbs** tetap berlaku di past tense — *was knowing, were believing* selalu salah.

---

## 3. Past Perfect

### Definisi
Menyatakan **aksi yang terjadi dan selesai sebelum aksi lain di masa lalu**. Past Perfect adalah "masa lalu dari masa lalu" — digunakan untuk menunjukkan urutan dua kejadian di masa lalu.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + had + Past Participle (V3)
```
> *She **had left** before I arrived.*
> *By 9 PM, he **had finished** all his work.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + had + not (hadn't) + V3
```
> *I **hadn't eaten** anything before the meeting.*

**Interrogative**
```
Had + subject + V3?
```
> ***Had** she **left** before you arrived?*

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aksi yang Selesai Sebelum Aksi Lain di Masa Lalu**
> *When I arrived, she **had already left**.*
> *(Dia pergi dulu → baru saya tiba)*

> *He **had finished** dinner before she **called**.*

**b. Aksi yang Selesai Sebelum Waktu Tertentu di Masa Lalu**
> *By the time the movie started, we **had found** our seats.*
> *By 2010, she **had lived** in three different countries.*

**c. Kondisi dalam Kalimat Conditional Type 3**
> *If I **had studied** harder, I would have passed the exam.*

**d. Reported Speech (Backshift)**
> *She said she **had finished** the report.* *(= "I have finished the report")*

---

### Signal Words
`before` · `after` · `when` · `by the time` · `already` · `just` · `never ... before` · `by + waktu lampau` · `once`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Tidak menggunakan Past Perfect saat urutan waktu penting**
- ❌ *When I arrived, she left.*  *(tidak jelas mana yang lebih dulu)*
- ✅ *When I arrived, she **had already left**.* *(jelas: dia pergi sebelum saya tiba)*

**2. Memakai Past Perfect untuk semua aksi di masa lalu**

Past Perfect **hanya diperlukan** jika ada dua kejadian di masa lalu dan kita perlu menekankan mana yang terjadi lebih dahulu. Jika urutannya sudah jelas dari kata *before/after*, Simple Past bisa digunakan.

> *She left **before** I arrived.* ✅ (Simple Past sudah cukup karena "before" menunjukkan urutan)
> *When I arrived, she **had left**.* ✅ (Past Perfect diperlukan karena tidak ada penanda urutan eksplisit)

**3. Bingung had + V3 dengan have/has + V3**
- **had + V3** → Past Perfect (masa lalu dari masa lalu)
- **have/has + V3** → Present Perfect (masa lalu yang relevan dengan sekarang)

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Frasa **"by the time"** hampir selalu membutuhkan Past Perfect untuk aksi yang sudah selesai duluan.
  > *By the time she arrived, the meeting **had already started**.*
- Dalam soal **Conditional Type 3**, Past Perfect wajib ada di klausa `if`: *If + subject + **had** + V3*.
- TOEFL sering menguji apakah kamu bisa membedakan **had + V3** vs **have/has + V3** — perhatikan konteks waktu kalimatnya.

---

## 4. Past Perfect Continuous

### Definisi
Menekankan **durasi suatu aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung sebelum titik tertentu di masa lalu** — fokus pada **proses** yang telah berjalan sebelum kejadian lain terjadi.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + had + been + verb-ing
```
> *I **had been waiting** for an hour before she arrived.*
> *They **had been working** all night when the power went out.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + had + not (hadn't) + been + verb-ing
```
> *He **hadn't been sleeping** well before the exam.*

**Interrogative**
```
Had + subject + been + verb-ing?
```
> ***Had** you **been waiting** long before the bus came?*

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aktivitas yang Berlangsung Terus-Menerus Sebelum Titik Waktu di Masa Lalu**
> *She **had been studying** for 6 hours before she took a break.*
> *By the time I joined, they **had been discussing** the issue for an hour.*

**b. Penyebab dari Kondisi di Masa Lalu**
> *He was exhausted because he **had been running** all morning.*
> *Her eyes were red because she **had been crying**.*

**c. Aktivitas yang Baru Saja Selesai Sebelum Kejadian Lain**
> *I **had been waiting** for 30 minutes when she finally showed up.*

---

### Signal Words
`for` · `since` · `all day / all morning` · `before` · `when` · `by the time` · `how long` · `the whole time`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Memakai bentuk ini dengan stative verbs**
- ❌ *She had been knowing him for years.*
- ✅ *She **had known** him for years.* → gunakan Past Perfect biasa

**2. Bingung dengan Past Perfect biasa**

| Kalimat | Makna |
|---|---|
| *I **had read** the report before the meeting.* | Fokus pada hasil — laporan sudah selesai dibaca |
| *I **had been reading** the report for an hour before the meeting.* | Fokus pada durasi proses membaca |

**3. Menggunakan Past Perfect Continuous tanpa konteks pembanding**

Tense ini selalu butuh **titik referensi** di masa lalu untuk dibandingkan. Tanpa itu, kalimat terasa menggantung.
- ❌ *I had been working.* *(tidak jelas — sebelum apa?)*
- ✅ *I **had been working** for hours **before** I finally ate.*

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Past Perfect Continuous **sangat jarang menjadi fokus utama** soal TOEFL, tapi muncul sebagai opsi pengecoh.
- Kunci: jika ada **durasi + titik waktu lampau** → Past Perfect Continuous. Jika hanya menekankan **fakta selesai** → Past Perfect.
- Sama seperti di present, **stative verbs dalam bentuk `-been + V-ing`** selalu salah.

---

## Ringkasan Perbandingan 4 Past Tenses

| Tense | Rumus | Fokus | Contoh |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Simple Past** | V2 | Aksi selesai di masa lalu, waktu spesifik | *She worked late yesterday.* |
| **Past Continuous** | was/were + V-ing | Aksi sedang berlangsung di masa lalu | *She was working at 9 PM.* |
| **Past Perfect** | had + V3 | Aksi selesai sebelum aksi lain di masa lalu | *She had worked there for 5 years before she quit.* |
| **Past Perfect Continuous** | had + been + V-ing | Durasi proses sebelum titik waktu di masa lalu | *She had been working for hours before she took a break.* |
