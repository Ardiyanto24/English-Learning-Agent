# Present Tenses

## Overview

Present tenses dalam bahasa Inggris bukan hanya tentang "sekarang". Setiap bentuk memiliki **fungsi spesifik** yang berbeda-beda. Kesalahan paling umum adalah memakai *Simple Present* untuk semua situasi. Panduan ini membahas **4 bentuk present tense** secara lengkap dan terstruktur.

---

## 1. Simple Present (Present Simple)

### Definisi
Menyatakan **fakta umum, kebiasaan, rutinitas, dan kebenaran universal**. Tidak selalu berarti kejadian yang sedang terjadi saat ini.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
I / You / We / They  →  base verb
He / She / It        →  base verb + -s / -es
```
> *She **works** every day. / They **eat** dinner at 7 PM.*

**Negative**
```
I / You / We / They  →  do not (don't) + base verb
He / She / It        →  does not (doesn't) + base verb
```
> *She **doesn't work** on weekends. / They **don't eat** late.*

**Interrogative**
```
Do    + I / you / we / they + base verb?
Does  + he / she / it       + base verb?
```
> *Does she **work** here? / Do they **eat** at home?*

**Aturan Ejaan -s / -es (Third Person Singular)**

| Kondisi | Aturan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| Kebanyakan verb | Tambah **-s** | work → works |
| Berakhir -ch, -sh, -x, -ss, -o | Tambah **-es** | teach → teaches, go → goes |
| Konsonan + y | Ganti **y → ies** | study → studies |
| Vokal + y | Tambah **-s** | play → plays |
| Irregular | Hafal | be → is, have → has |

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Kebiasaan & Rutinitas**
> *He **goes** to the gym every morning.*

**b. Fakta & Kebenaran Umum**
> *Water **boils** at 100°C. / The sun **rises** in the east.*

**c. Keadaan Permanen**
> *She **lives** in Jakarta. / I **work** as an engineer.*

**d. Jadwal Resmi (Scheduled Future)**
> *The train **leaves** at 9 AM tomorrow.*

**e. Instruksi & Petunjuk**
> *You **press** the button and **wait** for the light.*

**f. Narasi / Komentar Live**
> *He **passes** the ball — and **scores**!*

---

### Signal Words
`always` · `usually` · `often` · `sometimes` · `rarely` · `never` · `every day/week/month` · `on Mondays` · `in general` · `as a rule`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Lupa -s/-es untuk he/she/it**
- ❌ *She work hard.*
- ✅ *She **works** hard.*

**2. Pakai does + verb-s**
- ❌ *Does she works here?*
- ✅ *Does she **work** here?*

**3. Pakai Simple Present untuk aksi yang sedang berlangsung**
- ❌ *I eat right now.*
- ✅ *I **am eating** right now.*

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Soal TOEFL sering menjebak dengan **intervening phrase** antara subject dan verb. Fokus pada subject aslinya, bukan kata terdekat dengan verb.
  > *The quality of these products **is** excellent.* (subject = quality, bukan products)
- Kata seperti `always`, `usually`, `often` dalam kalimat adalah **sinyal kuat** Simple Present — pastikan bentuk verbnya sesuai dengan subject.
- Dalam **reading passage**, Simple Present sering muncul untuk menyatakan teori ilmiah, definisi, dan fakta — ini tidak memerlukan pembuktian waktu.

---

## 2. Present Continuous (Present Progressive)

### Definisi
Menyatakan **aksi yang sedang berlangsung saat ini**, **situasi sementara**, atau **rencana pasti di masa depan**.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + am / is / are + verb-ing
```
> *I **am working** now. / She **is studying** at the moment.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + am / is / are + not + verb-ing
```
> *I **am not working** today. / She **isn't studying** right now.*

**Interrogative**
```
Am / Is / Are + subject + verb-ing?
```
> ***Is** she **studying**? / **Are** they **coming**?*

**Aturan Ejaan -ing**

| Kondisi | Aturan | Contoh |
|---|---|---|
| Kebanyakan verb | Tambah **-ing** | work → working |
| Berakhir huruf -e | Hapus **e**, tambah **-ing** | write → writing |
| CVC + satu suku kata + vokal pendek | Gandakan konsonan + **-ing** | run → running, sit → sitting |
| Berakhir -ie | Ganti **ie → y** + ing | lie → lying |

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aksi yang Sedang Berlangsung**
> *She **is talking** on the phone right now.*

**b. Situasi Sementara**
> *He **is staying** at a hotel this week.*

**c. Perubahan & Tren**
> *The climate **is getting** warmer.*

**d. Rencana Pasti di Masa Depan**
> *We **are meeting** the client tomorrow at 10.*

**e. Kebiasaan Menjengkelkan (dengan "always") — Nada Emosional**
> *He **is always interrupting** me!*

---

### Signal Words
`now` · `right now` · `at the moment` · `currently` · `at present` · `these days` · `this week/month` · `Look!` · `Listen!`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Memakai stative verb dalam bentuk continuous**

Verb berikut **tidak boleh** dipakai dalam bentuk -ing karena menyatakan keadaan, bukan aksi:

| Kategori | Contoh Verb |
|---|---|
| Perasaan & Emosi | love, hate, like, want, need, prefer |
| Pikiran & Opini | know, believe, understand, remember |
| Kepemilikan | have (own), own, belong, possess |
| Persepsi | see, hear, smell, taste |
| Keadaan | be, seem, appear, exist, contain |

- ❌ *I am knowing the answer.*
- ✅ *I **know** the answer.*

**2. Beberapa verb bisa bermakna ganda**

| Kalimat | Makna |
|---|---|
| *I **have** a car.* | Kepemilikan → Simple Present |
| *I **am having** lunch.* | Aksi makan → Continuous ✅ |
| *She **looks** tired.* | Keadaan → Simple Present |
| *She **is looking** at me.* | Aksi melihat → Continuous ✅ |

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- TOEFL sering menguji **stative verbs** — pilihan jawaban yang menggunakan *is knowing*, *am understanding*, *are believing* selalu **salah**.
- Jika soal menyertakan kata `now`, `at the moment`, atau `currently`, hampir pasti jawabannya Present Continuous.
- Hati-hati dengan **future meaning** — kalimat seperti *"She is flying to Bali next week"* tidak salah, tapi TOEFL kadang membingungkan ini dengan Simple Future. Kuncinya: ada konteks rencana yang sudah pasti dan terorganisir.

## 3. Present Perfect

### Definisi
Menghubungkan **masa lalu dengan kondisi sekarang** — fokus pada **relevansi atau hasil** suatu kejadian, bukan kapan tepatnya terjadi.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
I / You / We / They  →  have + Past Participle (V3)
He / She / It        →  has  + Past Participle (V3)
```
> *I **have finished** the report. / She **has lived** here for 5 years.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + have / has + not + V3
```
> *I **haven't finished** yet. / She **hasn't called** me.*

**Interrogative**
```
Have / Has + subject + V3?
```
> ***Have** you **ever visited** Paris? / **Has** she **called**?*

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Pengalaman Hidup — tanpa waktu spesifik**
> *I **have visited** Paris. / She **has never seen** snow.*

**b. Hasil yang Masih Relevan Sekarang**
> *I **have lost** my keys.* *(dan sekarang tidak bisa masuk)*

**c. Situasi yang Dimulai di Masa Lalu dan Masih Berlanjut (FOR / SINCE)**
> *She **has worked** here **for** 10 years.* *(masih kerja di sini)*
> *I **have lived** in Jakarta **since** 2015.*

> **FOR** = durasi → *for 3 days, for a week, for years*
> **SINCE** = titik awal waktu → *since Monday, since 2010, since I was a child*

**d. Perubahan Sepanjang Waktu**
> *Technology **has changed** dramatically.*

**e. Baru Saja Terjadi (JUST)**
> *She **has just arrived**.*

**f. Belum Terjadi (YET)**
> *I **haven't finished** yet. / Has he called **yet**?*

**g. Sudah Terjadi (ALREADY)**
> *I **have already eaten**. / She **has already left**.*

---

### Signal Words
`ever` · `never` · `just` · `already` · `yet` · `recently` · `lately` · `so far` · `up to now` · `for` · `since` · `this week/month/year` *(periode yang belum selesai)*

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Memakai Present Perfect dengan waktu spesifik**
- ❌ *I have visited Paris **in 2019**.*
- ✅ *I **visited** Paris in 2019.* → gunakan Simple Past

**2. Bingung FOR vs SINCE**
- ❌ *I have lived here since 5 years.*
- ✅ *I have lived here **for** 5 years.*
- ❌ *I have lived here for 2019.*
- ✅ *I have lived here **since** 2019.*

**3. Memakai Simple Past padahal hasilnya masih relevan**
- ❌ *I lost my keys.* *(ambigu — bisa sudah ditemukan)*
- ✅ *I **have lost** my keys.* *(masih hilang sekarang)*

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Kata **`since`** dalam kalimat adalah sinyal paling kuat untuk Present Perfect. Jika ada `since`, verb utamanya hampir selalu `have/has + V3`.
- TOEFL sering menjebak dengan **waktu spesifik** seperti *yesterday, in 2010, last year* — ini **selalu** membutuhkan Simple Past, bukan Present Perfect.
- Kombinasi **`for` + durasi** bisa muncul di Simple Past juga (*I worked there for 5 years* = sudah tidak bekerja di sana). Bedakan konteksnya: jika masih berlanjut → Present Perfect.

---

## 4. Present Perfect Continuous

### Definisi
Menekankan **durasi atau proses berkelanjutan** dari aktivitas yang dimulai di masa lalu dan **masih berlangsung** atau **baru saja selesai** dengan efek yang terlihat sekarang.

---

### Struktur

**Positive**
```
Subject + have / has + been + verb-ing
```
> *I **have been working** for 3 hours. / She **has been studying** since morning.*

**Negative**
```
Subject + have / has + not + been + verb-ing
```
> *I **haven't been sleeping** well lately.*

**Interrogative**
```
Have / Has + subject + been + verb-ing?
```
> ***Have** you **been waiting** long? / **Has** she **been feeling** better?*

---

### Fungsi Utama

**a. Aktivitas yang Masih Berlangsung dengan Penekanan Durasi**
> *I **have been waiting** for you for an hour!*
> *How long **have** you **been learning** English?*

**b. Aktivitas Baru Saja Selesai dengan Bukti yang Terlihat**
> *You look tired. **Have** you **been running**?*
> *She **has been crying** — her eyes are red.*
> *It **has been raining** — the ground is wet.*

**c. Tindakan Berulang Selama Periode Tertentu**
> *I **have been calling** him all morning.*
> *They **have been arguing** a lot lately.*

---

### Signal Words
`for` · `since` · `all day / all morning / all week` · `lately` · `recently` · `how long` · `the whole time`

---

### Jebakan Umum ⚠️

**1. Memakai bentuk ini dengan stative verbs**
- ❌ *I have been knowing him for years.*
- ✅ *I **have known** him for years.* → gunakan Present Perfect biasa

**2. Bingung dengan Present Perfect biasa**

| Kalimat | Makna |
|---|---|
| *I **have read** the book.* | Buku sudah selesai dibaca — fokus pada hasil |
| *I **have been reading** the book.* | Proses membaca masih berjalan atau baru saja selesai |
| *She **has worked** here for 5 years.* | Fakta: sudah 5 tahun bekerja di sini |
| *She **has been working** here for 5 years.* | Menekankan: terus-menerus aktif selama 5 tahun |

---

### Tips TOEFL 🎯

- Present Perfect Continuous **jarang menjadi fokus utama** soal TOEFL, tapi sering muncul sebagai **pengecoh** melawan Present Perfect.
- Kunci membedakannya: jika soal menekankan **hasil/fakta** → Present Perfect. Jika menekankan **proses/durasi yang terasa** → Present Perfect Continuous.
- Jika ada **stative verb** di opsi jawaban dalam bentuk `been + V-ing`, itu **selalu salah**.

---

## Ringkasan Perbandingan 4 Present Tenses

| Tense | Rumus | Fokus | Contoh |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Simple Present** | V1 / V1+s/es | Kebiasaan, fakta, kebenaran umum | *She works every day.* |
| **Present Continuous** | am/is/are + V-ing | Sedang terjadi, sementara, rencana dekat | *She is working now.* |
| **Present Perfect** | have/has + V3 | Pengalaman, hasil relevan, durasi (for/since) | *She has worked here for 5 years.* |
| **Present Perfect Continuous** | have/has + been + V-ing | Proses berkelanjutan, durasi yang terasa | *She has been working since 9 AM.* |
